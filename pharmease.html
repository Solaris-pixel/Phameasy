Is my prescription data secure?

Yes. All documents are encrypted with AES-256, stored in HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Absolutely. No lock-in contracts. Cancel from your account settings at any time. If you cancel mid-cycle, you keep access until the period ends.

Do you accept insurance?

Family plan users can submit insurance claims directly. We currently work with over 200 providers across Nigeria and are expanding coverage continuously.

How does teleconsultation work?

Book a slot in-app and connect with a licensed physician via chat or video call. They can issue new prescriptions directly to your PharmEase account.
